Understanding the ISA Millionaire Calculator

This calculator helps you determine how long it will take to reach £1,000,000 in your ISA based on your annual contributions and expected investment returns.

Input Parameters:

  • Annual ISA Contribution: The amount you contribute to your ISA each year (maximum allowance currently £20,000).
  • Annual Nominal Return: The expected annual return on your investments before adjusting for inflation.
  • Annual Inflation Rate: The expected rate at which the purchasing power of money decreases over time.

Results Explained:

  • Years to £1 Million (Nominal Value): The number of years it will take for your ISA to reach £1,000,000 in nominal terms.
  • Years to £1 Million in Today's Value: The number of years it will take for your ISA to reach the equivalent of £1,000,000 in today's purchasing power, adjusted for inflation.

The chart displays both the nominal value of your ISA and its inflation-adjusted real value over time, allowing you to visualize your progress towards becoming an ISA millionaire.
